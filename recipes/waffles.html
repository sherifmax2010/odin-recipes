waffles

Description

With just basic pantry staples like flour, eggs, and milk, these waffles require very little prep. Home cooks of all levels praise this simple method for creating fluffy, crisp waffles every time. Allrecipes member Nikki Badzik shares, "They were crunchy on the outside, soft on the inside."

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon salt or to taste
  • 4 teaspoons baking powder
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 ½ cups warm milk
  • ⅓ cup butter, melted

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Mix flour, salt, baking powder, and sugar together in a large bowl; set aside. Preheat waffle iron to desired temperature.
  3. Beat eggs in a separate bowl; stir in milk, butter, and vanilla.
  4. Pour milk mixture into flour mixture; beat until blended.
